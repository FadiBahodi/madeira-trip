Recommended 4-Day Shape

Day 1 — Peaks

Sunrise at Pico do Areeiro + PR1.2 to Pico Ruivo summit. Or PR8 Sao Lourenco if peaks are fogged.

Day 2 — Waterfalls & Forest

PR6 + PR6.1 at Rabacal. Fanal ancient forest. Porto Moniz pools. North coast drive.

Day 3 — Deep Green Levada

PR9 Caldeirao Verde (tunnels + waterfall) OR PR10 Levada do Furado. Santana. Wine tasting evening.

Day 4 — Gardens + Sunset

Palheiro Gardens (camellias peak!). Market. Cabo Girao. Sunset espetada at Estreito de Camara de Lobos.
