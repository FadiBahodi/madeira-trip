Madeira in 60 Seconds

Never been? Here's everything you need to understand before reading the day plans.

🛣

What's a Levada?

Irrigation channels built centuries ago to carry water from the wet north to the dry south. They carved flat paths along mountainsides that are now Madeira's signature hiking trails — flat, scenic, following water through tunnels and forests. Most famous hikes follow levadas.

🥾

What's a PR Trail?

PR = Pequena Rota (Short Route). Madeira's official numbered hiking trails (PR1 through PR50+). PR trails require a reservation (€4.50 each) booked on SIMplifica. Book the night before. Screenshot the QR code — there's no signal at trailheads.

🏔

Island Geography

South coast (Funchal) = sunny, urban, your base. North coast = wild, green, rainier. Peaks (1800m) = above the clouds, freezing at dawn. West (Rabaçal, Porto Moniz) = waterfalls, forests, lava pools. The island is small — nothing is more than 1.5h drive from Funchal.

⛅

Weather Strategy

Madeira has multiple microclimates. It can be foggy at the peaks and sunny on the coast simultaneously. Check webcammadeira.com every morning. Clear peaks = do summits. Cloudy peaks = do coast or forest. March: 17-20°C coast, 3-8°C peaks.

🚗

Driving

You need a rental car. Mountain roads with narrow tunnels, hairpin turns, and stunning views. Get automatic transmission (trust us). March: €15-25/day. Get SCDW insurance (zero excess). Local companies (Insular Car, AutoRentaCar) are cheaper than internationals.

🍷

Poncha + Espetada

Two things you can't get anywhere else. Poncha: rum + honey + citrus, deceptively strong (~50% ABV masked by sweetness). Always drink fresh, NEVER bottled. Espetada: beef on laurel-wood skewers hung vertically. THE Madeira food experience. Designate a driver for poncha nights.

Parking

  • Free parking: Reid's, Cliff Bay, Quinta da Casa Branca, Quinta Jardins, Quintinha São João, ARTS IN, Three House, all outside-Funchal hotels
  • Paid (~€15/day): Savoy Palace, Castanheiro, most central hotels
  • No on-site: Barceló Old Town, Sé Boutique, Jaca Hostel (need public garage)
  • Street parking in Funchal center is nearly impossible and never free

Breakfast

  • Outstanding: Quinta Jardins do Lago, Reid's, Castanheiro, Cliff Bay, Quinta da Casa Branca, ARTS IN
  • Good: Barceló, Porto Santa Maria, Quintinha São João, Quinta do Furão
  • Big but meh: Hotel Alto Lido (quantity over quality, bad coffee)
  • Hotel breakfast in Madeira is generally excellent. Take it if included — saves time for early hiking starts

Split Stay?

  • Safest bet: Funchal base all 4-5 nights. Nowhere is more than 90 min drive
  • Best split: 3 nights Funchal + 1 night Calheta/Saccharum (sunset) or Porto Moniz (pools)
  • Closest "escape": Pestana Churchill Bay in Câmara de Lobos — 10 min from Funchal, different vibe
  • Do NOT split into more than 2 bases on a 5-night trip — packing/unpacking eats into short trips
